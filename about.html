About the Game

METAL SLUG MAKES AN EXPLOSIVE RETURN! Developed by Leikir Studio and published by Dotemu, Metal Slug Tactics reimagines the classic arcade franchise as a turn-based tactical strategy game. Players build squads, upgrade abilities, and complete challenging missions while enjoying the fast-paced action and humor that made the series famous.

Game Features

  • Classic Metal Slug pixel art
  • Turn-based tactical combat
  • Build powerful squad combinations
  • Upgrade heroes with unique abilities
  • Battle against General Morden's army

Gameplay

Players command iconic members of the Peregrine Falcon Squad, carefully positioning each character on the battlefield while making use of unique weapons, abilities, and synchronized attacks. Every mission requires tactical thinking and smart decision-making.
